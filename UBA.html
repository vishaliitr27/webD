About UBA Club

The UBA Club at IIT Roorkee is dedicated to contributing towards the development of rural areas through the Unnat Bharat Abhiyan initiative. Our mission is to address challenges faced by villagers and farmers, providing sustainable solutions for their betterment.

Our Work

Through various projects and initiatives, we actively engage with rural communities, identifying problems and implementing solutions. Our focus areas include agriculture, healthcare, education, and infrastructure development.

Join Us

If you are passionate about making a positive impact in rural areas, we welcome you to join the UBA Club. Together, we can work towards creating a better and sustainable future for our fellow citizens in rural India.
